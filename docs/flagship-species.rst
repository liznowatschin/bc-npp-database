Flagship Species
================

These species are the initial v1.0.0a and v1.0.0 foundation set for
low-growing urban native plant work in coastal British Columbia.

Forbs and bulbs
---------------

* Achillea millefolium
* Camassia quamash
* Camassia leichtlinii
* Cerastium arvense
* Collinsia parviflora
* Eriophyllum lanatum
* Fragaria virginiana
* Lupinus bicolor
* Plectritis congesta
* Potentilla gracilis
* Prunella vulgaris ssp. lanceolata
* Ranunculus occidentalis
* Sisyrinchium idahoense
* Triteleia hyacinthina
* Viola adunca

Grasses
-------

* Danthonia californica
* Festuca roemeri
* Koeleria macrantha

Sedges
------

* Carex inops
* Carex tumulicola

First gold-standard record
--------------------------

The recommended first gold-standard species record is Achillea millefolium
because it is common, well studied, widely available, urban tolerant, drought
tolerant, valuable to pollinators, and useful as a template for many data
sections.
