Source Policy
=============

Evidence tiers
--------------

Tier 1: peer-reviewed literature
   Use for pollinator associations, specialist bee relationships, seed ecology,
   restoration outcomes, germination ecology, and ecological-function evidence.

Tier 2: government and academic sources
   Use for taxonomy, native status, conservation status, range, habitat,
   floristic descriptions, and ecosystem suitability.

Tier 3: regional expert and practitioner sources
   Use for propagation, germination practices, cultivation notes, nursery
   production, seed handling, and practical establishment guidance.

Approved Tier 3 source
----------------------

Satinflower Nurseries is approved as a regional practitioner source when used
with appropriate attribution and confidence labeling.

Excluded sources
----------------

The following source must not be used anywhere in the project:

.. code-block:: text

   https://vancouver.ca/files/cov/vancouver-gri-planting-guidelines.pdf

This exclusion applies to citations, source-attribution fields, scoring
rationale, species notes, and documentation.

Source hierarchy
----------------

Prioritize sources in this order:

* Government and conservation-status sources.
* Regional floras and herbarium-backed databases.
* Peer-reviewed literature.
* Conservation organizations.
* Botanical gardens and universities.
* Native plant nurseries and seed suppliers for commercial and cultivation data.
